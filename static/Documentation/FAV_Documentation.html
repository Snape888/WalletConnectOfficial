1. Introduction to FAV

Lending and earning in the decentralized finance space is being redefined. FAV, or Fixed Asset Value, provides both borrowers and liquidity providers with innovative products that stand out in the DeFi landscape.

2. FAV Loans

2.1 Overview

FAV Loans empower borrowers to access under-collateralized loans, allowing them to lock in the value of their favorite tokens and plan their financial future with certainty. With FAV Loans, users can borrow a desired amount[a] by only putting up a fraction of the amount as collateral. The unique mechanism ensures that the price of the borrowed token remains fixed for the term of the loan.

2.2 Use Cases[b]

  • Jane's Bullish Bet on ETH: Jane believes that Ethereum (ETH) will see significant price appreciation in the next five years. She borrows 10 ETH through FAV Loans, locking in its current price. Over the loan term, Jane makes her fixed USD payments. By the end of her loan term, ETH has tripled in value, but thanks to FAV Loans, Jane has acquired her 10 ETH at the price she locked in five years ago, resulting in significant savings.

  • Mike and the Transferable NFT: Mike took out a FAV Loan but found himself in a tight financial spot. Instead of defaulting, he sold his loan NFT on the secondary market. The new holder continued the repayments, and Mike was able to manage his financial challenges without losing his collateral.[c]

2.3 Benefit[d]s

  • Lock-In Token Prices: Secure the current market price of your desired tokens, safeguarding against future market volatility.
  • Borrow more with Less: Break free from over-collateralized norms. Pledge only a fraction of your desired borrowing amount.[e][f]
  • Transferrable Obligations: Every loan comes with an NFT, allowing borrowers the flexibility to transfer their loan obligations.

2.4 How It Works[g]

  • Initiating a Loan: Choose your desired token, decide the loan amount, and determine the loan term (from 1 to 30 years).
  • Setting the Collateral: Depending on the loan term, deposit a fraction of the loan amount as collateral.
  • Repayments: Adhere to a fixed regular payment schedule in USD value. Failing to make a payment results in an immediate default.
  • Completion: Upon fulfilling all repayments, the borrower can claim the full loan amount.

2.5 FAV Loan NFTs

FAV Loans go beyond traditional lending. With each loan, borrowers receive a transferable NFT, representing their loan obligations. This NFT can be traded, giving flexibility in managing financial responsibilities.

  • Listing the NFT: Borrowers can list their NFTs on supported platforms for trading.
  • Transferring Ownership: Once traded, the new NFT holder assumes the responsibility of the loan's repayments.

3. FAV Earn

3.1 Overview

FAV Earn redefines the liquidity provision experience. Dive into a single-sided liquidity pool, eliminating the challenges of impermanent loss found in multi-asset pools. Beyond just providing liquidity, FAV Earn rewards you through a unique rebasing[h] mechanism. Instead of manually claiming reward[i]s, enjoy the convenience of synthetic stable coins that rebase upwards, all denominated in USD for added stability and predictability.

3.2 Use Cases

  • Sarah's Passive Earnings: Sarah, a crypto enthusiast, decided to provide liquidity to FAV Earn. By depositing her USDC[j] into the conversion contract, she received synthetic stable tokens. Over time, she observed her token balance increasing automatically without her intervention, thanks to the rebasing mechanism[k]. After a year, she had a significant increase in her holdings, all without a single manual claim transaction.[l]
  • Tom's Diversified Portfolio[m]: Tom, a seasoned liquidity provider, was tired of managing multi-asset pools and facing impermanent loss. He turned to FAV Earn and started providing liquidity in various ERC20 lending vaults. This allowed him to diversify his portfolio, earn from different synthetic stable tokens, and avoid the hassles of impermanent loss.

3.3 Benefits

  • Innovative Earnings: Enjoy passive income through the unique rebasing [n]mechanism of synthetic stable tokens.
  • Priority Redemptions: Liquidity providers are prioritized in token redemptions, ensuring a smooth and beneficial experience.
  • Diversified Opportunities: With synthetic stable tokens tied to different ERC20 lending vaults, providers can diversify their portfolio.

3.4 How It Works

  • Choosing a Vault: Decide which ERC20 token lending vault you want to provide liquidity to.
  • Providing Liquidity: Deposit a stablecoin like USDC into the conversion contract[o] of your chosen vault and receive synthetic stable tokens in return.
  • Earnings through Rebasing: Synthetic stable tokens automatically[p] rebase, ensuring liquidity providers earn without claiming[q] actively through transactions.
  • Redemption: When you decide to exit, redeem your synthetic stable tokens for the original stablecoins. The redemption process ensures liquidity providers are prioritized.
  • Monitoring and Exiting: Keep an eye on your earnings and decide the right time to exit, ensuring you maximize your returns.[r]

3.5 Synthetic Stable Tokens

Synthetic stable tokens are unique to FAV Earn. These tokens are pegged to the dollar[s] and represent the liquidity provided to a specific ERC20 lending vault. As these tokens are vault-specific, they cannot be traded or interchanged with tokens from another vault[t]. Their primary utility is rebasing[u], which means they increase in number as rewards accrue, eliminating the need for manual claims.

4. Guide [UI screenshots missing]

4.1 Borrowers

Embarking on the FAV Loans journey is straightforward. Here's a step-by-step guide:

  • Token Selection: Begin by choosing your preferred token to borrow.
  • Determining Loan Amount & Term: Decide the amount you wish to borrow and select a loan term ranging from 1 to 30 years.
  • Deposit Collateral: Based on your chosen loan term, deposit the required collateral amount.
  • Making Repayments: Ensure you make the fixed regular USD payments to avoid defaulting on your loan.
  • Claiming the Borrowed Amount: Once all repayments are completed, claim the full borrowed amount.

4.2 Liquidity Providers

Liquidity providers play a crucial role in the FAV ecosystem. Here's a guide to provide liquidity and earn rewards:

  • Choosing a Vault: Decide which ERC20 token lending vault you want to provide liquidity to.
  • Depositing Stablecoin: Provide liquidity by depositing stablecoins like USDC into the conversion contract of your chosen vault.
  • Receiving Synthetic Stable Tokens: Upon depositing, you receive synthetic stable tokens specific to the chosen vault, which rebase automatically, allowing you to earn passively.
  • Monitoring Earnings: Witness your token balance increase over time due to the rebasing mechanism.
  • Redemption: When you're ready to exit, redeem your synthetic stable tokens for the original stablecoins.[v][w]

4.3 Arbitrageurs

Arbitrageurs help in ensuring that asset prices within FAV remain close to real market prices. Here's how you can participate in arbitrage:

  • Depositing Asset[x]s: As an arbitrager, deposit assets into the system.
  • Waiting Period: Once deposited, there's a waiting period before you can claim stablecoins in return for your assets.
  • Claiming Stablecoins: After the waiting period, claim the stablecoins based on the prevailing conversion rate[y].

4.4 Handling Defaults

In the event a borrower defaults on their repayment, here's how the system handles it:

  • Immediate Default: The system immediately recognizes a default if a borrower misses a payment.
  • Transfer of Funds: Post default, the funds that made up the loan are sent back to the lending pool.
